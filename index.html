About

Welcome to DYDTIL!

We are a team of young leaders from three different nations who came together to develop a project that addresses the challenges faced by youths.

Through skills acquisition in entrepreneurship, technology and literacy, youths in Botswana, Cameroon and Indonesia will be well equipped with the knowledge and training they need to embark in careers in technology, enabling them to meet their own needs, empower themselves and others and address challenges in their communities by incorporrating technological means. Subsequently, as impact is created, we will advance to other parts of the world.

  • Our Mission

  • Why Us?

Curbing youth unemployment through inclusive technopreneurship training and literacy, skill acquisition in ICT so they can be up to speed with the digital advancements in today's era.

Obviously, we are not the first of our kind. We are different in that we look for the youth in need and not the youth in need looking for us. We are not focused on making any profits for ourselves, but rather we are focused on ensuring our trainees and learners create sources of income for themselves and to develop their communities in the long run.
