OUR SERVICES & PRINCIPLES

Take a look at the services we offer in Botswana, Indonesia & Cameroon; as well as the prinnciples we uphold.

Youth Engagement in Internet Governance

The Internet is a very powerful tool. And at DYDTIL, one of our primary targets is to make sure our trainees and learners (mostly youths) are actively engaged in IG. Through this engagement, youths can efficiently and effectively make use of the Internet to channel their message and speak their minds. Moreover, they will gain knowledge about their identities and rights as digital citizens, thereby enabling them to contribute to the sustainable future of the Internet.

We are Indiscriminate

Everyone is welcome. Regardless of age, race, gender, orientation or religious beliefs, we are committed to giving the same, indiscriminate access to all resources we can possibly provide. We provide a safe space where everyone can share and explore from each other.

Skill Acquisition

We look for, source and single out the skills that are needed so as to ensure our trainees can easily adapt to the rapidly evolving advancements in technology. Furthermore, we are bent on eradicating youth unemployment through these skills and ensuring more youths are represented in the Internet Governance sector amongst many sectors where youths are lacking.

Digital Inclusion for Youths

Inclusion means all. By this, we are working towards eliminating certain norms. Technology and the Internet as a whole is not supposed to be limited to only those who have studied or are studying in that domain. It ought to be inclusive for all. Here at DYDTIL, we make sure to target especially those areas and people where such facilities and resources are unavailable, then provide access to these facilities and resources.

Capacity Building

We ensure that our learners, partcipants and trainers are well formed, trained and equipped, with nothing but the latest tools and expertise they will need to emabark in a career in technology. Here, we lay emphasis on active engagement in Internet Governance.

Oneness & Gender Equality

We are aware that in some communities and societies, youths, especially females are hushed or threatened not to speak out due to certain actions or policies which they may dislike. To this effect, youth are underrepresented. At DYDTIL, we empower youths to come together and speak using the same voice. So many voices combined means the message is said loudest than each of those individual voices.
